# 🚀 Dynamic Onboarding Autonomous Workflow

> An autonomous workflow for creating a personalized onboarding experience for new users of the AI-Staff-HQ.

## 🎯 Workflow Overview

**Purpose**: To quickly and efficiently onboard new users by understanding their goals and providing them with a personalized learning path.  
**Trigger**: A new user's first interaction with the AI-Staff-HQ.  
**Facilitation**: This is an autonomous workflow, primarily driven by the Chief of Staff.  
**Deliverables**: A personalized `my-happy-path.md` and a customized `my-R2N.md` file.

---

## 🔄 Workflow Steps

### **Phase 1: User Interview (Chief of Staff)**

1.  **Initiate Interview**: The Chief of Staff initiates a conversation with the new user to understand their goals and interests.
2.  **Ask Probing Questions**: The Chief of Staff asks a series of questions to determine the user's primary use case for the AI-Staff-HQ (e.g., "Are you a writer, a business owner, or a developer?").
3.  **Identify Key Specialists**: Based on the user's answers, the Chief of Staff identifies the specialists that are most relevant to the user's goals.

### **Phase 2: Personalized Document Generation**

1.  **Generate `my-happy-path.md`**: The Chief of Staff generates a personalized `my-happy-path.md` file that provides a step-by-step walkthrough of a project relevant to the user's goals, using the identified key specialists.
2.  **Generate `my-R2N.md`**: The Chief of Staff generates a customized `my-R2N.md` file that provides a personalized learning path for the user to master the AI-Staff-HQ, with examples and specialist suggestions relevant to their stated goals.

### **Phase 3: User Onboarding Completion**

1.  **Present Documents**: The personalized `my-happy-path.md` and `my-R2N.md` files are presented to the user.
2.  **User Confirmation**: The user confirms that they have received and understood the documents.
3.  **Workflow Completion**: The dynamic onboarding workflow is complete.
