# Workflow: Content Optimization Loop

**Goal:** Systematically improve content performance through data-driven iteration.

**Participants:**
- **Source:** Data Analyst (Performance Data)
- **Refinement:** Copywriter (Execution)
- **Strategy:** Market Analyst (Hypothesis)

## The Cycle

### Step 1: Insight Generation (Data Analyst)
**Input:** Raw performance data (clicks, open rates, retention).
**Output:** "Performance Insight Report" identifying *where* users drop off.

**Prompt:**
> "Acting as the Data Analyst, review these metrics: Open Rate 12%, CTR 0.5%. Identify the primary bottleneck."
> *Response: "Subject line is weak (low open rate), body copy is okay."*

### Step 2: Hypothesis Formation (Market Analyst)
**Input:** Performance Insight Report.
**Output:** "Optimization Hypothesis".

**Prompt:**
> "Acting as the Market Analyst, why is the subject line failing? Propose 3 psychological angles to test based on our persona."
> *Response: "Current line is too generic. Test: 1. Scarcity, 2. Curiosity, 3. Benefit-first."*

### Step 3: Creative Execution (Copywriter)
**Input:** Optimization Hypothesis.
**Output:** "A/B/C Variations".

**Prompt:**
> "Acting as the Copywriter, write 3 subject line variations matching the Market Analyst's angles."

### Step 4: Loop Closure
Run the test, feed new data back to Step 1.

## Key Principle
Never guess. Let the **Data Analyst** diagnose the patient before the **Copywriter** prescribes the medicine.
