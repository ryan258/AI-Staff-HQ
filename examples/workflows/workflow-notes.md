# How to Use the Workflow Transcripts

These transcripts are storytelling tools that expose the orchestration layer between human operator and AI specialists. When the examples are filled in, focus on:

- The intent behind each prompt and the adjustments made after receiving responses.
- How context is passed between specialists without overloading the model.
- The retrospective section that separates what worked from what needs refinement.

Leverage the structure to document your own workflows. The goal is to capture decision-making, not just inputs and outputs.
