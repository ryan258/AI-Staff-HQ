## **🚀 Getting Started with Your Workforce**

### **Phase 1: Your First Specialist**

1. **Start Simple**: Use the `new-staff-member-template.md` to create your first custom specialist.
2. **Get Help**: Use the `Prompt Engineer` to help you design your new specialist's prompts and capabilities.
3. **Test**: Test your new specialist on a simple task.

### **Phase 2: Your First Team**

1. **Build a Team**: Create a second specialist that complements your first one.
2. **Coordinate**: Use the `Chief of Staff` to coordinate a project involving your two new specialists.
3. **Create a Workflow**: Adapt an existing workflow from the `workflows` directory to use your new team.
