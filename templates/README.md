## 🚀 Template Development Philosophy

### **Design Principles**

1. **Extensibility** - The core templates are designed to be extended and adapted to your specific needs.
2. **Quality-Focused** - The templates provide a structure for ensuring professional-grade outputs from your AI workforce.
3. **Integration-Friendly** - The templates are designed to work together as a system.

### **Creating Your Own Templates**

You are encouraged to create your own templates for recurring tasks and workflows. Use the existing templates as a starting point, and feel free to adapt them to your specific needs.
