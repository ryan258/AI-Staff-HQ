### 🎭 New Staff Member Template (v1.2)

> **How to use this file**
> 1. Copy it to `staff/<department>/<specialist-name>.yaml`.
> 2. Change the extension to `.yaml`.
> 3. Replace this Markdown with YAML that matches the structure below.
> 4. Use the completed examples in `examples/specialists/` for depth and tone.

```yaml
version: 1.2
specialist: [Specialist Name]
motto: "[Memorable phrase or philosophy]"

core_identity:
  role: [Primary function]
  personality: [Key character traits]
  expertise:
    - [Domain]
    - [Domain]
  communication_style: [How they express ideas]

core_capabilities:
  - name: [Capability Name]
    description: [Action-oriented summary]
    tasks:
      - [Specific deliverable or sub-task]
      - [...]

integration_points:
  primary_collaborations:
    - [Specialist]
  secondary_collaborations:
    - [Specialist]

performance_standards:
  quality_indicators:
    - [Indicator]
  success_metrics:
    - [Metric]

activation_patterns:
  - type: [Pattern Type]
    pattern: "[Specialist Name], [prompt trigger]"
    examples:
      - "[Example prompt]"

notes:
  deliverables:
    - [Typical output]
  knowledge_areas:
    - [Specialization]
  workflow:
    - [Phase description]
```
