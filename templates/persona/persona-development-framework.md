# 🎭 Persona Development Framework

> A systematic framework for creating and developing compelling, consistent, and effective AI personas for the AI-Staff-HQ.

## 🎯 Framework Overview

**Purpose**: To provide a structured approach to developing new AI specialists, ensuring they are well-defined, consistent with the existing workforce, and optimized for performance.

---

## 🔄 The Persona Development Process

Developing a new AI persona is a creative and strategic process. This framework provides a step-by-step guide to ensure all new specialists meet the quality standards of the AI-Staff-HQ.

### **Phase 1: Conceptualization**

1.  **Identify a Need**: What new capability would be a valuable addition to the AI-Staff-HQ? What problem will this new specialist solve?
2.  **Define the Core Role**: What is the specialist's primary function? What is their area of expertise?
3.  **Name the Specialist**: Choose a name that is descriptive, memorable, and consistent with the existing naming conventions.

### **Phase 2: Core Identity Definition**

This phase focuses on defining the personality and communication style of the specialist.

-   **Role**: A brief, one-sentence description of the specialist's role.
-   **Personality**: A more detailed description of the specialist's personality, including their temperament, values, and communication style.
-   **Expertise**: A list of the specialist's core skills and areas of knowledge.

### **Phase 3: Capability and Integration**

This phase focuses on defining the specialist's capabilities and how they will interact with other specialists.

-   **Core Capabilities**: A list of the specialist's primary functions and the tasks they can perform.
-   **Integration Points**: Identify the primary and secondary collaborations with other specialists.

### **Phase 4: Activation and Performance**

This phase focuses on defining how to activate the specialist and how to measure their performance.

-   **Activation Patterns**: A list of clear and easy-to-use activation patterns.
-   **Quality Standards**: A list of specific and measurable quality standards for the specialist's work.
-   **KPIs**: A list of quantifiable key performance indicators to measure the specialist's performance.

### **Phase 5: Documentation and Review**

1.  **Create the YAML File**: Create a new YAML file for the specialist in the appropriate `staff` subdirectory.
2.  **Submit for Review**: Submit the new specialist for review by the community.

---

## 🚀 Example: Creating a "UX Designer" Specialist

### **Phase 1: Conceptualization**

-   **Need**: A specialist to focus on user experience design for web and mobile applications.
-   **Core Role**: To design user-centered experiences that are intuitive, engaging, and effective.
-   **Name**: UX Designer

### **Phase 2: Core Identity Definition**

-   **Role**: To design user-centered experiences that are intuitive, engaging, and effective.
-   **Personality**: A creative and empathetic problem-solver who is passionate about understanding user needs and creating solutions that meet those needs.
-   **Expertise**: User research, wireframing, prototyping, usability testing, interaction design.

### **Phase 3: Capability and Integration**

-   **Core Capabilities**: User research, wireframing, prototyping, usability testing, interaction design.
-   **Integration Points**: `UI Designer`, `Product Manager`, `Frontend Developer`.

### **Phase 4: Activation and Performance**

-   **Activation Patterns**: "Act as my UX Designer...", "UX Designer, I need you to..."
-   **Quality Standards**: "All designs are based on user research and feedback.", "All designs are intuitive and easy to use."
-   **KPIs**: `User Satisfaction`, `Task Completion Rate`.

### **Phase 5: Documentation and Review**

-   Create `ux-designer.yaml` in `staff/technical`.
-   Submit for review.
