# Dynamic Onboarding Interview

> A template for the Chief of Staff to interview a new user and create a personalized onboarding experience.

## Interview Questions

**Chief of Staff:** "Welcome to the AI-Staff-HQ! To help you get started, I'd like to ask you a few questions to understand your goals. This will help me create a personalized onboarding experience for you.

1.  **What is your primary role or profession?** (e.g., writer, developer, entrepreneur, student)
2.  **What do you hope to achieve with the AI-Staff-HQ?** (e.g., write a book, build a web application, start a business, learn a new skill)
3.  **Are there any specific areas you are interested in exploring?** (e.g., creative writing, web development, marketing, personal productivity)

Based on your answers, I will generate a personalized `my-happy-path.md` and a customized `my-R2N.md` to help you get started."
