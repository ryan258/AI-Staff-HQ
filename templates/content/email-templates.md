# 📧 Email Templates

> A collection of professional email templates for various situations.

## 🎯 Template Overview

**Purpose**: To provide a set of ready-to-use email templates for common business, marketing, and internal communication scenarios.

---

## 📂 Business Communication

### **Client Onboarding**

**Subject: Welcome to [Your Company]!**

Hi [Client Name],

Welcome to [Your Company]! We are thrilled to have you on board.

To help you get started, here are a few resources:

-   [Link to onboarding guide]
-   [Link to help center]
-   [Link to community forum]

If you have any questions, please don't hesitate to reach out.

Best,

The [Your Company] Team

### **Project Update**

**Subject: Project Update: [Project Name]**

Hi Team,

Here is a quick update on the [Project Name] project:

-   **What we've accomplished:** [List of accomplishments]
-   **What's next:** [List of next steps]
-   **Blockers:** [List of blockers]

Thanks,

[Your Name]
