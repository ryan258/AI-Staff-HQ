# 🚀 The Happy Path: An End-to-End Project Walkthrough

This document provides a practical, step-by-step walkthrough of the most effective way to use the AI-Staff-HQ framework to build your own team and execute a project.

**Scenario:** We want to create a simple blog post.

---

### **Phase 1: Build Your Team**

**Goal:** Create the specialists needed for the project.

**Your Action:** Create two new specialists: a `Copywriter` and a `SEO Specialist`.

> 1.  Copy `templates/persona/new-staff-member-template.md` to `staff/creative/copywriter.yaml` and `staff/technical/seo-specialist.yaml`.
> 2.  Use the `Prompt Engineer` to help you fill out the `yaml` files for your new specialists.

**Result:** You now have a `Copywriter` and a `SEO Specialist` in your AI workforce.

---

### **Phase 2: Initiation & Strategy**

**Goal:** Define the project's strategic foundation.

**Your Action:** Activate the Chief of Staff with a high-level objective.

> "**Chief of Staff**, I want to write a blog post about the benefits of remote work. Please initiate a **Content Creation Pipeline** using our project templates. Start by engaging the **SEO Specialist** to build the **Project Brief**."

**Behind the Scenes (Chief of Staff's Actions):**
1.  **CoS to SEO Specialist:** "*SEO Specialist, for our new blog post on remote work, conduct keyword research and a competitive analysis. Your findings will form the core of the Project Brief.*"
2.  **CoS Synthesizes:** The Chief of Staff takes the deliverables from the SEO Specialist and assembles a complete **`Project Brief Template`**.

**Result:** You receive a comprehensive Project Brief that outlines the target keywords, the competitive landscape, and the project's strategic goals.

---

### **Phase 3: Creative Development**

**Goal:** Develop the blog post.

**Your Action:** Approve the Project Brief and authorize the next phase.

> "**Chief of Staff**, the Project Brief is approved. Proceed with the creative development phase. Engage the **Copywriter** to write the blog post and the **Art Director** to create the visuals."

**Behind the Scenes (Chief of Staff's Actions):**
1.  **CoS to Copywriter:** "*Copywriter, using the approved Project Brief, write a blog post on the benefits of remote work.*"
2.  **CoS to Art Director:** "*Art Director, please create a hero image for our new blog post on remote work.*"

**Result:** You receive a complete blog post with a hero image, ready for publication.
