# 🧠 Cognitive Load Optimization

> A guide to managing your mental resources and optimizing your cognitive load.

## 🎯 Overview

**Purpose**: To provide a set of strategies and techniques for managing cognitive load and improving mental performance.

---

## 🤔 Understanding Cognitive Load

Cognitive load refers to the total amount of mental effort being used in the working memory. There are three types of cognitive load:

-   **Intrinsic Cognitive Load**: The inherent difficulty of the task.
-   **Extraneous Cognitive Load**: The mental effort required to process information that is not directly relevant to the task.
-   **Germane Cognitive Load**: The mental effort required to process information, construct schemas, and automate procedures.

## 🚀 Strategies for Optimizing Cognitive Load

-   **Reduce Intrinsic Load**: Break down complex tasks into smaller, more manageable steps.
-   **Reduce Extraneous Load**: Eliminate distractions and irrelevant information.
-   **Increase Germane Load**: Use techniques like spaced repetition and retrieval practice to build strong mental models.
