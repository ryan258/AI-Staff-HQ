# 📚 Learning Frameworks

> A collection of learning frameworks that accelerate knowledge acquisition and mastery.

## 🎯 Framework Overview

**Purpose**: To provide a set of structured techniques and methodologies for learning new skills and acquiring knowledge more effectively.

---

##  Feynman Technique

The Feynman Technique is a mental model for learning that involves explaining a concept in simple terms.

1.  **Choose a concept** you want to learn.
2.  **Teach it to a child**. Explain the concept in simple terms that a child can understand.
3.  **Identify your knowledge gaps**. When you struggle to explain the concept, you have identified a gap in your understanding.
4.  **Review and simplify**. Go back to the source material and fill in your knowledge gaps. Then, simplify your explanation even further.

## 🔁 Spaced Repetition

Spaced repetition is a learning technique that involves reviewing information at increasing intervals.

-   **How it works**: When you first learn something, you review it frequently. As you become more familiar with the information, you review it less frequently.
-   **Why it works**: Spaced repetition takes advantage of the spacing effect, which is the phenomenon whereby learning is greater when studying is spread out over time.

## 🎯 Deliberate Practice

Deliberate practice is a highly structured form of practice that is designed to improve performance.

-   **Key components**:
    -   **Specific goals**: Each practice session should have a specific goal.
    -   **Intense focus**: You should be fully focused on the task at hand.
    -   **Immediate feedback**: You should get immediate feedback on your performance.
    -   **Constant refinement**: You should constantly be looking for ways to improve.
