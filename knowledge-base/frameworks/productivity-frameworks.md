# ⚡ Productivity Frameworks

> A collection of productivity frameworks that multiply output while maintaining quality.

## 🎯 Framework Overview

**Purpose**: To provide a set of structured systems and methodologies for improving efficiency, managing priorities, and achieving goals.

---

## 🚀 Getting Things Done (GTD)

GTD is a five-step method for managing your tasks and projects.

1.  **Capture**: Collect everything that has your attention.
2.  **Clarify**: Process what you have captured and decide what to do with it.
3.  **Organize**: Put your tasks and projects in the right place.
4.  **Reflect**: Review your system regularly.
5.  **Engage**: Get to work on your tasks.

##  MATRIX: The Eisenhower Matrix

The Eisenhower Matrix is a simple tool for prioritizing tasks based on urgency and importance.

-   **Urgent & Important**: Do it now.
-   **Important, Not Urgent**: Schedule it.
-   **Urgent, Not Important**: Delegate it.
-   **Not Urgent, Not Important**: Delete it.

## 🍅 The Pomodoro Technique

The Pomodoro Technique is a time management method that uses a timer to break down work into intervals, traditionally 25 minutes in length, separated by short breaks.

1.  **Choose a task**.
2.  **Set a timer for 25 minutes**.
3.  **Work on the task until the timer rings**.
4.  **Take a short break (5 minutes)**.
5.  **After four pomodoros, take a longer break (15-30 minutes)**.
