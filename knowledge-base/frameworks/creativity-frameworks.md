# 🎨 Creativity Frameworks

> A collection of creativity frameworks to generate breakthrough innovations systematically.

## 🎯 Framework Overview

**Purpose**: To provide a set of structured techniques and methodologies for generating new ideas, solving problems, and fostering innovation.

---

## 💡 SCAMPER

SCAMPER is a creative thinking technique that uses a set of seven verbs to prompt new ideas.

-   **S**ubstitute: What can you substitute? What can be replaced?
-   **C**ombine: What can you combine? What can be merged?
-   **A**dapt: What can you adapt? What else is like this?
-   **M**odify: What can you modify? What can be added or subtracted?
-   **P**ut to another use: What else can it be used for?
-   **E**liminate: What can you eliminate? What can be removed?
-   **R**everse: What can you reverse? What can be rearranged?

##  изобретательство (TRIZ)

TRIZ is a problem-solving methodology based on the study of patterns of invention in the global patent literature.

-   **40 Inventive Principles**: A set of 40 principles that can be used to solve technical problems.
-   **Contradiction Matrix**: A matrix that helps to identify which of the 40 principles are most likely to be useful for a given problem.
-   **Separation Principles**: A set of principles for separating contradictory requirements.

## 🎩 Six Thinking Hats

Six Thinking Hats is a technique for looking at a problem from different perspectives.

-   **White Hat**: Focus on the facts and data.
-   **Red Hat**: Focus on emotions and intuition.
-   **Black Hat**: Focus on the potential problems and risks.
-   **Yellow Hat**: Focus on the potential benefits and opportunities.
-   **Green Hat**: Focus on new ideas and creative solutions.
-   **Blue Hat**: Focus on the process and the big picture.
