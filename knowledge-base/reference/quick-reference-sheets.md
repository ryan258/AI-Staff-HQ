## 🚀 Specialist Activation Quick Cards

### **Art Director**

-   **Core Function**: Visual strategy and brand aesthetics.
-   **Activation Patterns**:
    -   `"Act as my Art Director..."`
    -   `"Art Director, I need a visual concept for..."`
-   **Key Questions to Ask**:
    -   What is the target audience?
    -   What is the desired mood and tone?
    -   What are the key messages to convey?

### **Copywriter**

-   **Core Function**: Persuasive writing and brand voice.
-   **Activation Patterns**:
    -   `"Act as my Copywriter..."`
    -   `"Copywriter, write a headline for..."`
-   **Key Questions to Ask**:
    -   Who is the target audience?
    -   What is the call to action?
    -   What is the unique selling proposition?

## 🗺️ Workflow Execution Checklists

### **Content Creation Pipeline**

1.  [ ] **Strategy & Ideation**: Define goals, research topics, and create a content brief.
2.  [ ] **Production & QA**: Draft content, create visuals, and conduct an editorial review.
3.  [ ] **Distribution & Promotion**: Publish content, optimize for SEO, and promote on social media.
4.  [ ] **Analysis & Optimization**: Monitor performance and identify opportunities for improvement.

### **Brand Development Workflow**

1.  [ ] **Strategic Foundation**: Initiate the project, conduct market research, and develop a brand strategy.
2.  [ ] **Identity Development**: Create a visual identity and messaging framework.
3.  [ ] **System Documentation**: Create brand guidelines and an implementation plan.
4.  [ ] **Validation & Refinement**: Gather stakeholder feedback and make final optimizations.

## 🤔 Decision-Making Quick Guides

### **The 5 Whys**

-   **Purpose**: To get to the root cause of a problem.
-   **How to Use**: Ask "Why?" five times to uncover the underlying cause of a problem.

### **The Eisenhower Matrix**

-   **Purpose**: To prioritize tasks based on urgency and importance.
-   **How to Use**: Categorize tasks into four quadrants:
    -   **Urgent & Important**: Do it now.
    -   **Important, Not Urgent**: Schedule it.
    -   **Urgent, Not Important**: Delegate it.
    -   **Not Urgent, Not Important**: Delete it.
