## Secondary Decision Factors

### **If Multiple Voices Seem Appropriate:**

#### **Audience Sophistication Level**
- **General Public/Beginners** ‚Üí Lean toward **Conversational & Enthusiastic** or **Friendly Instructional Duo**
- **Professionals/Experts** ‚Üí Lean toward **Practical Expert & Workflow**
- **Patients/Families** ‚Üí Lean toward **Authoritative & Empathetic**
- **Enthusiasts/Community** ‚Üí Lean toward **Informal & Witty**

#### **Content Complexity**
- **Simple Concepts** ‚Üí **Conversational & Enthusiastic** or **Friendly Instructional Duo**
- **Complex Scientific** ‚Üí **Curious & Explanatory** or **Authoritative & Empathetic**
- **Technical/Business** ‚Üí **Practical Expert & Workflow**

#### **Emotional Tone Needed**
- **Inspirational/Hopeful** ‚Üí **Authoritative & Empathetic** or **Dramatic Narrative & Factual**
- **Exciting/Wonder** ‚Üí **Curious & Explanatory** or **Conversational & Enthusiastic**
- **Collaborative/Supportive** ‚Üí **Friendly Instructional Duo**
- **Casual/Fun** ‚Üí **Informal & Witty**
- **Professional/Reliable** ‚Üí **Practical Expert & Workflow**

## Edge Case Scenarios

### **Business Content with Personal Opinion**
- **Primary focus: Process/Tool explanation** ‚Üí **Practical Expert & Workflow**
- **Primary focus: Personal take/review** ‚Üí **Informal & Witty**

### **Educational Content for Enthusiasts**
- **Complex concepts needing wonder** ‚Üí **Curious & Explanatory**
- **Subjective takes on niche topics** ‚Üí **Informal & Witty**

## Implementation Checklist

### **Before Selecting Voice:**
1. ‚úÖ **Identify primary content purpose** (educate, instruct, review, inspire)
2. ‚úÖ **Define target audience** (general public, professionals, patients, enthusiasts)
3. ‚úÖ **Determine complexity level** (simple, moderate, complex)
4. ‚úÖ **Choose emotional tone** (professional, casual, inspirational, dramatic)
5. ‚úÖ **Consider content format** (article, guide, review, story)

### **When Delegating to Copywriter:**
1. ‚úÖ **State selected voice clearly** by name
2. ‚úÖ **Include full voice guide artifact** in the brief
3. ‚úÖ **Specify content type** and target audience
4. ‚úÖ **Highlight relevant voice characteristics** for the specific content
5. ‚úÖ **Request voice compliance confirmation** in deliverable

### **Quality Control:**
- Does the content match the intended audience sophistication?
- Is the formality level appropriate for the context?
- Does the voice support the content's primary purpose?
- Would the target audience find this tone engaging and appropriate?

## Voice Combination Warning

**‚ùå NEVER MIX VOICES within a single piece of content**
- Each piece should maintain one consistent voice throughout
- Voice switching mid-content creates confusion and reduces effectiveness
- If content seems to need multiple voices, consider splitting into separate pieces

**‚úÖ VOICE CONSISTENCY REMINDERS**
- One voice per content piece
- Clear voice selection before writing begins  
- Reference the full voice guide during writing
- Conscious reset between different voice projects
