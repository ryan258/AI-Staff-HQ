# 🗣️ Voices Directory

> _A collection of voice and tone guidelines for your AI workforce._

## 🎯 The Role of Voice

Voice is the personality of your AI specialists. It's what makes them unique and memorable. A well-defined voice ensures that your specialists communicate in a consistent and authentic way, which is crucial for building trust and rapport with your users.

This directory contains a collection of voice guides that you can use as a starting point for developing the voices of your own custom specialists. You can also use these guides to ensure that your specialists' communication is consistent with your brand's voice and tone.

## 📚 Core Voice Guides

- **[VOICE-GUIDE.md](VOICE-GUIDE.md)**: A consolidated guide containing a variety of voice archetypes.
- **[voice_selection_decision_tree.md](voice_selection_decision_tree.md)**: A decision tree to help you select the right voice for your specialist.
