# Retrospective Report: Aether Brew Launch

**Project:** Aether Brew Brand Launch
**Date:** 2025-10-08
**Facilitator:** Chief of Staff

---

### **Phase 1: KPI Adjudication**

**Adjudicator:** Actuary

*   **KPI 1: Achieve 5,000 newsletter subscribers pre-launch.**
    *   **Verdict:** `MET`
    *   **Rationale:** Final pre-launch subscriber count was 6,214, exceeding the target by 24.3%.

*   **KPI 2: Secure 1,000 founding member subscriptions in the first month.**
    *   **Verdict:** `NOT MET`
    *   **Rationale:** The project secured 812 founding members in the first month, achieving 81.2% of the target.

*   **KPI 3: Maintain a customer satisfaction score of 4.9/5.0 or higher.**
    *   **Verdict:** `MET`
    *   **Rationale:** The average customer satisfaction score across all feedback channels was 4.95/5.0.

**Project ROI Calculation:**
*   **Verdict:** `POSITIVE`
*   **Rationale:** The revenue from 812 founding members in the first month exceeded the total project resource cost by 18%.

---

### **Phase 2: Key Learnings**

**What Worked Well:**
*   **Strategy-Creative Handoff:** The `Project Brief` provided a very strong foundation, allowing the `Art Director` and `Copywriter` to execute quickly and with minimal revisions. The process was highly efficient.
*   **Brand Voice:** The "Precisely Crafted" voice, as executed by the `Copywriter`, resonated extremely well with the target audience, as evidenced by social media comment analysis.

**What Could Be Improved:**
*   **Conversion Funnel:** The `Actuary`'s verdict on KPI 2 (Founding Members) suggests a weakness in the final step of the conversion funnel. The landing page successfully gathered interest (KPI 1), but did not convert that interest to sales as effectively as projected.
*   **Resource Allocation:** The `Toolmaker` was under-utilized. Their capability to build more complex, interactive web experiences could have been leveraged to create a more engaging landing page, potentially improving conversion.

### **Phase 3: Actionable Improvements for Next Project**

1.  **Refine Conversion-Focused Copy:** For the next project, the `Copywriter` will dedicate more time to A/B testing calls-to-action and the final checkout flow copy.
2.  **Integrate `Toolmaker` into Creative Phase:** On future web projects, the `Toolmaker` will be included in the initial `Creative Brief` phase alongside the `Art Director` to consult on interactive possibilities.
3.  **Update `Brand Builder` Profile:** The success of the positioning statement for a tech-savvy audience should be noted. The `Head Librarian` will be tasked to log this successful pattern.
